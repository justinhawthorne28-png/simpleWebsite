My Role in the Tech Industry

I am currently majoring in Integrated Information Technology.

Trends That Inspire Me

  • Human-Centered Tech: Designing systems that prioritize accessibility, empathy, and ethical use—especially in support and training environments.
  • Edge Computing: Bringing processing closer to the source for faster diagnostics, reduced latency, and smarter IoT integration.
  • Remote Support Tools: Empowering technicians to troubleshoot across distances.
  • Digital Twin Technology: Creating virtual replicas of hardware systems to simulate performance, troubleshoot remotely, and optimize upgrades.
  • Tech for Good: Using IT solutions to address social challenges—like digital equity, sustainability, and mental health support through smart platforms.

Certifications & Learning Goals

I'm currently pursuing certifications in CompTIA and exploring ethical AI development, network security, and system architecture. I believe in lifelong learning and staying ahead of industry shifts.

Why This Matters

Technology is more than tools—it's about people. My mission is to make tech accessible, reliable, and empowering for everyone I support.
