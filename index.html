Integrity

  1. Living honestly and ethically, even when no one is watching.
  2. Taught by family and reinforced through school and work experiences.
  3. Builds credibility and respect; it's the foundation of strong character.
  4. Without integrity, relationships and reputations crumble.

Hard Work

  1. Believing in others and being someone others can rely on.
  2. Developed through friendships and teamwork—especially when stakes were high.
  3. Essential for collaboration, leadership, and emotional safety.
  4. Without trust, communication breaks down and progress stalls.

Trust

  1. Putting in consistent effort and pushing through challenges.
  2. Inspired by role models who achieved success through perseverance.
  3. Drives achievement, builds resilience, and earns respect.
  4. Without hard work, goals remain dreams and potential goes untapped.

Faith

  1. Belief in something greater—whether spiritual, personal, or moral.
  2. Rooted in religious upbringing or personal reflection during tough times.
  3. Provides strength, hope, and purpose during uncertainty.
  4. Without faith, it's easy to feel lost or overwhelmed by adversity.
